Welcome to Pro Prima Enterprise : Traders and Suppliers of Laboratory Equipment & Chemicals

PRO PRIMA ENTERPRISE established in 2003 and has since gained a name for us as traders and suppliers of laboratory equipment & chemicals. We supply our products to various government departments, universities, college, private & research institutions.

In order to keep in place with the advancements of today's technology & the ever growing demands, we decided to increase our products line and thus included raw materials and minerals such as barites, feldspar, quartz, iron ore & bentonite. Our goal is to find and offer our customer the highest quality and best designed products with excellent after purchase technical support.

The greatest compliment we receive is when our customers look to us an information resources as well as a source of quality laboratory equipment. PRO PRIMA ENTERPRISE is committed to providing you with on time delivery and affordable prices of Laboratory equipments , Glasswares and our range of products to meet your every need.
