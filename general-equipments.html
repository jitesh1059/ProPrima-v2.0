Temperature Control Devices

Temperature control devices are an integral component of any laboratory and are needed in almost any experimental workflow. Pro Prima supplies multiple devices to accommodate your temperature control needs.

Pro Prima can provide general-use temperature-controlled water bath and a microprocessor-controlled dry heating bath with blocks for centrifuge and microcentrifuge tubes.



Centrifuges

A centrifuge is a laboratory device that is used for the separation of fluids, gas or liquid, based on density. Separation is achieved by spinning a vessel containing material at high speed; the centrifugal force pushes heavier materials to the outside of the vessel.

A centrifuge is an integral component of almost every experimental workflow. We offers two research-quality centrifuges and a PCR tube adaptor to accommodate your laboratory needs.



Mixing Devices

Rocking platforms, vortexers, and tube rollers provide the most commonly used mixing actions for general laboratory use and most experimental workflows.

Rocking platforms provide excellent options for continuous agitation of stains and reagents on gels and blots while conserving bench space and maximizing capacity. Vortexer is a general-purpose mixer for your laboratory needs. The tube roller offers horizontal and vertical mixing angles including end-over-end mixing, tumbling, rotating, and rocking for samples in a variety of tube sizes
